Ala' Khalifeh
+962 6 5664750 ext. 112

In addition to his role as Managing Partner and Head of Infrastructure and Projects at Khalifeh & Partners, Ala' has managed a number of renewable energy, water, and sustainable infrastructure cases. In particular, he is renowned for his niche experience advising on and navigating all aspects of energy projects in Jordan and throughout the region, having led the team of lawyers advising on key developments in Jordan's renewable energy sector. In connection with his expertise in Jordan energy law during the past five (5) years, many of which have been PPP transactions, Ala' is currently advising on a number of complex, large-scale projects including the Attarat Oil Shale direct burning project with an approximate value of USD $2.2 billion, as well as a number of wind and solar PV projects. In addition to his experience in project finance, Ala' has also been involved in the corporatization of the Water Authority of Jordan's activities in Amman and Aqaba, and he has also acted as the issuer's legal counsel on a number of sovereign bond issues with a total value of approximately USD $4 billion based on Reg. S and Rule 144/4.
