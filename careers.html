Why Join us?

Khalifeh & Partners is a dynamic and growing firm. Advising clients in an evolving market keeps us at the cutting edge of legal and commercial developments — regionally and beyond.

As we continue to grow, we are always looking to bring exceptional lawyers into our practice groups. We value professionalism, academic strength, technical excellence, commercial awareness, and clear, confident communication.

Whether you're just starting out or bringing years of experience, the decisions you make now will shape both your career and your life.

At Khalifeh & Partners, we've built a working environment grounded in principles that support excellence, growth, and long-term fulfilment. These values define what you can expect from us — and what we'll empower you to achieve.

How It Works

You’ll join us as part of a structured program built to make the most of your time here. Over the course of your internship, you’ll gain exposure to:

  • Foundational legal skills – From taking instructions and meeting clients to drafting memos and working in teams, you’ll develop the day-to-day skills essential for a future in law.
  • Core practice areas – You’ll learn how our key practice groups operate—who they are, how they’re structured, the matters they handle, and your place within the team.
  • Firm culture – We believe our culture sets us apart. Throughout your internship, you’ll be encouraged to take part in the events, activities and everyday interactions that shape life at Khalifeh & Partners.
  • Our people – You’ll work alongside lawyers at every level of the firm. We’re a collaborative firm, and you’ll find people are generous with their time, ready to share insights and help you succeed.
  • The business of law – You’ll be introduced to the systems and structures that support the firm—our technology, research tools, financial systems, and internal policies.
